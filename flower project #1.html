Our Goal 目標



Why choose us?


Our non-profit aims to bring joy to solitary elders by providing them with plants/flowers. Scientific research has shown that the beauty and fragrance of flowers have a positive impact on mental health. We aim to raise funds through crowdfunding, sponsorships, online donations, and partnerships with local businesses. Our goal is to distribute these gifts to lonely elders through senior living facilities or community centers. Join us in making a positive impact on our community and bringing smiles to those who need it most.




為何選擇我們?

繁花妍的目標是利用植物與花卉來為孤獨老人帶來快樂及幸福。據研究顯示，放置植物在家裡能提升人們的幸福感及鎮靜效果。每個盆栽都會是我們透過科學研究與數據來篩選出來，會對人體帶來好處的品種。我們將會透過線上捐款籌款，贊助，及與當地企業合作來籌集資金，並發送植物們給獨居老人。如果你也有興趣給獨居老人們種下一顆愛的種子的話，也邀請你一起加入我們的行列，一起共同行動，讓世界變成一個更好，更有影響力的地方。

We Are...

Our non-profit organization was founded by two high school students who wanted to make a difference in the lives of solitary elders in our community. We believe that everyone deserves to feel loved and appreciated, especially those who may feel isolated and forgotten. By giving flowers and plants to these elders, we hope to bring a little bit of joy and brightness into their lives. We believe that small acts of kindness can have a big impact, and we are excited to continue spreading love and positivity through our organization.

我們是...

繁花妍是一個是由兩位熱愛研究生物的高中生創立的非盈利機構，初衷是想在社會裡種下一顆愛的種子，讓它散播關愛與祝福給大家，尤其是獨居老人。我們希望能利用我們所擁有的科學知識來為他人帶給溫暖，尤其是被孤立或遺忘的人。我們始終相信不管是年或少，每個人都值得被世界好好對待，及被愛。
